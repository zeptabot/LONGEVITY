Sleep Hygiene Tactics

The 11-Point Checklist

  • 1. Darkness 2 hrs before bed
  • 2. No smart devices 1 hr before
  • 3. No food 3 hrs before sleep
  • 4. 30 min outdoor daylight (AM)
  • 5. Cool Room (~18°C)
  • 6. Hot Bath/Shower
  • 7. Fixed wake-up time
  • 8. If sleepless: change room, boring activity
  • 9. Avoid stress/news < 1hr before
  • 10. Wind down: Write worries, Rosary, Meditation
  • 11. Target: 8-9 hours in bed

Nutrition Tactics

I. Control Energy

  • TRE (Time Restricted): 12pm - 8pm window.
  • Restrictions: No sugar, no ultra-processed foods.

II. Control Macronutrients

A. Protein

  • Target: 2.2 g/kg (Range 2.2-3.7)
  • Source: Animal > Plant

B. Carbs

  • Type: Fibrous, non-refined, whole grain only.
  • Avoid Spikes: Monitor via CGM.

Target: 100 ± 15 mg/dL

C. Fats

MUFA (50-55%): BEST Olive Oil, Avocados.

SFA (15-20%): Minimize Butter/Lard.

PUFA (Rest): Fish, Seeds.

Omega-3 Protocol

  • Goal: Increase EPA/DHA.
  • Sources: Fatty fish (Salmon) or Fish Oil.
  • Avoid: High Omega-6 seed oils.
  • Target: 8-12% RBC [Test: Omega-3 Index]
